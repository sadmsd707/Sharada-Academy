Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

At Sharada Academy Baramati, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Camera Permissions & Usage

Our application requests access to your device's Camera. This permission is strictly used to allow teachers and students to scan OMR answer sheets for practice exams. Image data processed during OMR scanning is analyzed locally on the device to calculate scores and is not stored or shared with external parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic information required for the proper functioning of the learning management system:

  • Account Data: Student names, parent contact numbers, teacher IDs, passwords.
  • Academic Data: Attendance records, announcement logs, and practice test scores.
  • OMR Scans: Images of OMR sheets used for grading (processed in real-time).

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is utilized solely to deliver and improve educational services:

  • To monitor academic progress and generate performance reports.
  • To keep parents updated regarding their child's attendance and test grades.
  • To broadcast classroom announcements and share resources.

3. Data Security and Services

We use industry-standard security measures (including Google Firebase services) to protect your account information and academic history. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Contacting Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or data handling practices, please contact us at:
